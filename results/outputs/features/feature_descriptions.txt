DIABETIC RETINOPATHY FEATURE DESCRIPTIONS
==================================================

brightness_mean:
  Average pixel intensity across image (medical: overall exposure)

contrast_std:
  Standard deviation of pixels (medical: image clarity)

red_dominance:
  Red channel dominance ratio (medical: hemorrhage indicator)

green_vessel_density:
  Vessel density from green channel (medical: vascular network)

texture_variance:
  Laplacian texture variance (medical: lesion complexity)
